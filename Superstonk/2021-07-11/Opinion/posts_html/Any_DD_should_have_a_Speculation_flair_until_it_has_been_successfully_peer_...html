Title: Any DD should have a Speculation flair until it has been successfully peer reviewed

Author: PWNWTFBBQ

Post URL: https://www.reddit.com/r/Superstonk/comments/oic6c9/any_dd_should_have_a_speculation_flair_until_it/

I have posted a few analysis in the past, and quite a few of them were wrong. I used the flair DD because there isn't much definitive difference between "Possible DD" and "DD". Also, I think the flair "DD" causes apes to assume it is immediately THE TRUTH. To prevent false information from entering into superstonk, any submitted DD should have a "Speculation" flair until peer reviewed for accuracy. The "Possible DD" and "DD" flair should be removed and only mods should have the power to grant it.
