Just a heads up that there have been some transactions involving the Gnosis Safe/Proxy 0x10b that owns the GameStop NFT contract in the last 12 hours.

0x61a then updated the Gnosis/Proxy contract 0x10b to remove 0x850aa0b86b8aa76b95cef283bcb2e7c008c7202b as an owner, and add 0xaf89f1430faed758435c3ca457a0ff9014ce6015 as an owner.

There is no transaction history for the new owner 0xaf, and the only transaction 0x850 ever performed was to transfer ownership of the ENS registration/token for GME.eth to the Gnosis Safe.

The most recent Event changing the Gnosis Safe contract was on July 16, when the address that created the Gnosis Safe was removed as an owner.

TL/WTF/DR: Small happenings with the contract that owns the GameStop NFT contract: an owner was removed and a new one added. Zero speculation on why/what it means, but it's the first movement from these addresses since July 16.
