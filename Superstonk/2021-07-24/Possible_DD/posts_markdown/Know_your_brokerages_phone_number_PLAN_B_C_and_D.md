Lately we've all seen the weird sus shit going on like internet outages, influencer channel shutdowns, and general fuckery. I don't know if any of that has to do with the MOASS, but what I do know is that we haven't seen everything.

In times like these, it helps to have a backup plan B, C, and D! At minimum, you should take your graphite-filled mini tree and physically write down your brokerage's customer service number on a piece of tree sliver. Do your own research, write down the important shit, and keep copies so you know what your exit strategy is and don't panic when shit goes down.

For some reference, here are a list of brokerage numbers - find your brokerage, write down the number, memorize it, tattoo it on your ass, whatever! But make sure you're not just relying on your smartphone or interwebs at all times.

**Important note:** I have not personally called or verified any of these numbers myself. I just gathered them online. Please test and validate yourselves and/or look it up directly from your brokerage's website.

As usual, not financial advice.

EDIT 1: As some other apes have pointed out, when you call you will likely need to give your account information, SSN, and/or your password. Please also make sure that you have your account number written down on paper as well so you'll have it when you need it!
