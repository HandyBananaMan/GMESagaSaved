I keep seeing this idea posted that Ryan Cohen issued new shares of GameStop so, get this, shorts have a chance to cover so that GameStop could say that they offered the shorts a way to prevent a market collapse due to MOASS.

I’d just like to remind everyone the board of directors Fiduciary duty is to us, the share holders. Not to the people shorting the stock or the SEC or the DTCC.

Maybe I will stand to be corrected here. However I can’t fathom why this theory is even considered plausible, let alone has almost 10k upvotes on the front page.
