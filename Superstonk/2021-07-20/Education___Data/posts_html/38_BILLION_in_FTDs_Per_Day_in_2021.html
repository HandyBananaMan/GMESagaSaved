Title: $3.8 BILLION in FTDs Per Day in 2021

Author: WhatCanIMakeToday

Post URL: https://www.reddit.com/r/Superstonk/comments/oogjmm/38_billion_in_ftds_per_day_in_2021/

Seriously WTF!

According to the SEC Fail To Deliver Data, if you sum up the FTDs every day and calculate the value of them (# of FTDs for a stock that day x $ of the stock that day), the average dollar value of FTDs each day for 2021 is $3,853,138,714 ($3.853 BILLION). EVERY DAY.

The lowest was $1.9B on Jan 5 with the highest being $11.3B on June 21st.
